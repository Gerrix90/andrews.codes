Philosophy

A "User First" Approach

The web is one of the most important mediums that businesses can operate in. Users have very high expectations from destinations they choose to do business with. There's a nearly endless list of things that you could and should be doing to optimize your strategy and increase growth. Everyday, new trends, technologies, and ways of engaging your customers become available. It's my job to assess what's out there, and impliment solutions responsibly to best meet my clients goals and help maintain their users trust.

Intuitive Design

One of my favorite aspects of building websites and apps has to do with layout. It's so important that you present your users with the information they need right away. That can be much easier said than done, especially when you have many goals that you're trying to meet. I can work with you to better understand who's using your services and where you might be losing them.

Collaborative Coding

I am extremely particular about the software I use, and how I use it. There are thousands of solutions that can accomplish the same task. It's easy to get caught up in the short term and take the quickest, or cheapest route. The concept of "technical debt" is one that a lot of developers worry about and many business owners realize only once it's too late. I work with my clients to help them understand their aspirations in both long and short term, and choose the most appropriate set of technologies to impliment or create.

Technologies

I use a variety of popular technologies, frameworks and libraries to achieve the best quality in my work, efficently and with the support of some of the largest developer communities around.

Professional

I have over 6 years of experience building websites & applications. I began simply to make my own unique MySpace themes as far back as 2006, using not much more than static HTML, CSS, and copy & paste JavaScript. Soon after my interests expanded and I picked up PHP and began exploring frameworks such as Drupal, and more recently WordPress. I picked up Node.js around mid 2015 and over time have become extremely familiar with the Javascript ecosystem. I now mostly work with Angular, and with a few other related technologies that have proven to scale well and meet high demands. I work well on teams both large and small, or even on my own.

My focus has mostly been involved with designing user interfaces that can meet web accessibility, compatibility, and security standards. It is also very important to me that information is organized and presented clearly, with intent and solid design principles in mind. I leverage my skills in Web Development, Graphic Design, and various creative outlets to achieve those goals.
